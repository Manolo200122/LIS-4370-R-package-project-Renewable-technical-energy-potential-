renewable-technical-potential

Overview

This vignette shows how to explore US renewable energy technical potential by state using the usre_df dataset from the renewablepossibilities package.

Top 10 states by rooftop solar potential

usre_df %>%
slice_max(rooftop_pv_gwh, n = 10) %>%
ggplot(aes(x = reorder(state, rooftop_pv_gwh),
y = rooftop_pv_gwh)) +
geom_col() +
coord_flip() +
labs(
x = "State",
y = "Rooftop PV potential (GWh)",
title = "Top 10 states by rooftop solar technical potential"
)
